<p><img src="https://img-blog.csdn.net/20161102115843783?watermark/2/text/aHR0cDovL2Jsb2cuY3Nkbi5uZXQv/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70/gravity/Center" alt="" referrerPolicy="no-referrer" class="img-fluid"><br></p>
<p>下載最新的wampserver 3.0.6，進行正常的安裝，如果出現提示電腦出現缺少vcruntime140.dll，請安裝我下面的操作：</p>
<p>1.把<span style="font-size:13.3333px;">wampserver 3.0.6<span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;">進行解除安裝</span></span><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;">：</span><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;">開始選單-&gt;控制面板-&gt;程式/解除安裝程式，進行解除安裝，當然，你也可以使用其他類似軟體管家的工具進行解除安裝。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;">2.<span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;">這裡提供 64位的vc++2015，連結: http://pan.baidu.com/s/1pLaRTRd 密碼: un85  。<span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;">32位的請自行到http://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=30679</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;">3.把下載的<span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;">vc++2015安裝，無腦式安裝，一直按next就好。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;">4.把<span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;">vcruntime140.dll</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;">解壓檔案，把32位的vcruntime140.dll複製到：C:\Windows\SysWOW64裡，在把，zhuce.bat複製</span><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;">C:\Windows\SysWOW64</span><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;">，雙擊執行；當你看到：註冊成功的字樣，說明vrcuntime140.dll已經註冊成功了。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;">5.把<span style="font-size:13.3333px;">下載最新的</span><span style="font-size:13.3333px;">wampserver
 3.0.6重新安裝，</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-size:13.3333px;">6.如果遇到<span style="font-size:13.3333px;">wampserver

<p><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-family:arial, '宋體', sans-serif;font-size:14px;line-height:28px;"><span style="font-size:13.3333px;"><span style="font-size:13.3333px;"><strong><span style="color:rgb(51,51,51);font-family:Arial;font-size:14px;line-height:26px;">找到Apache安裝目錄下conf目錄下的httpd.conf檔案</span><br></strong></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
